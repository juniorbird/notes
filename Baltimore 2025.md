## Things to do
### Fells Point
![Pasted image 20250808194917](./Pasted%20image%2020250808194917.png)
Fells Point is one of the oldest parts of Baltimore, with many buildings dating to the 18th century. It's right next to the harbor and will be just a few minutes from our hotel

- [Thames Street Oyster House](https://www.thamesstreetoysterhouse.com/)
- Walk Thames Avenue & Main Street & Waterfront
- [Broadway Market](https://broadwaymarketbaltimore.com/)
### Inner Harbor
![Pasted image 20250808195042](./Pasted%20image%2020250808195042.png)
The inner harbor is a pedestrian mall in the old Baltimore harbor, just a couple of minutes from where we'd be staying.
- [National Aquarium](https://aqua.org/)
- [Science Center](https://www.mdsci.org/)
- [Ships](https://historicships.org/explore)
### Mt. Vernon
![Pasted image 20250808195221](./Pasted%20image%2020250808195221.png)
A beautiful old area of Baltimore with the best restaurants and some great museums, walking distance from where we'd be staying.
- [Walters Museum](https://thewalters.org/)
- Washington Monument
- [Babe Ruth Birthplace & Museum](https://baberuthmuseum.org/)
- Mt. Vernon Walking Tour
- [Basilica of the Assumption](https://www.americasfirstcathedral.org/)
### Federal Hill
![Pasted image 20250808195443](./Pasted%20image%2020250808195443.png)
Next to the Harbor, this area has great views and Baltimore's most famous market, but would be a long walk or short Uber of where we're staying.
- [Ft. McHenry](https://www.nps.gov/fomc/index.htm)
- [Lexington Market](https://www.lexingtonmarket.com/)
	- [Faidley's](https://www.faidleyscrabcakes.com/)
### North Baltimore
![Pasted image 20250808195549](./Pasted%20image%2020250808195549.png)
This is where I grew up. We'd really have to get a car to go there. Mostly residential but has (like all parts of the city) its own market, plus an excellent museum.
- [BMA](https://artbma.org/)
	- If we do that, JHU and home as well
- [Belvedere Market](https://belvederesquare.com/)
